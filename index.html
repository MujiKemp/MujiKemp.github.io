Research Interests

I am interested in human-centered AI and multi-modal generative models. I focus on societal value alignment in AI-generated content by large language models and diffusion models, including values such as diversity, equity, and inclusion, controlled through precise human interactions. I also study how agents perceive their environment through multi-modal inputs, hierarchically collaborate or compete in decision-making processes, and collectively generate actions with augmentations.

Human Centred Agent: societal AI, social value alignment, multi-modality, large language models
Landable & Scalable Generative AI: diffusion models, efficiency, disentanglement
Responsible AI: control, reliability, alignment
Applications: audio, music, sports, computer vision, natural language processing, healthcare, education
